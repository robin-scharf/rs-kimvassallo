There are things that move differently in a group than in one-to-one work. Being witnessed by others who recognise what you are saying — that, on its own, is a piece of the medicine.

Groups run for short cycles (typically six to eight sessions) around a theme. Past groups have explored belonging, family of origin, and life-stage transitions.

## How they work

- Small groups of six to eight participants
- Facilitated weekly sessions, in person in Maastricht
- A short conversation first to check fit
