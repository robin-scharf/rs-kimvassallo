International teams carry a particular kind of stress: time zones, language fatigue, cultural friction, distance from family. I work with organisations who want to take this seriously rather than treat it as background noise.

## What I offer

- Workshops on burnout, communication, and team transitions
- Group supervision for managers and people leaders
- One-to-one coaching for employees, contracted through the organisation

Get in touch and we can design something that fits your team.
