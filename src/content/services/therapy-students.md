Student life adds a particular weight: high expectations, a new language, a homesickness that is not always allowed in.

These sessions are designed around the rhythm of academic life. They are confidential, focused, and held at a sliding rate where possible.

## Often helpful for

- Anxiety, low mood, burnout
- Adjustment, homesickness, isolation
- Relationships with family, friends, partners
- Identity questions that show up when you are far from home
