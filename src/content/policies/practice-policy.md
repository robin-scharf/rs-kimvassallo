## Cancellation

Please give at least 24 hours' notice. Sessions cancelled with less notice are charged in full.

## Payment

Invoices are sent at the end of each month and are due within 14 days.

## Complaints

If you are unhappy with anything in our work, please tell me first. If we cannot resolve it together, you have the right to raise a complaint with the NIP.
