I am an accredited psychologist and a Gestalt therapist in training. My practice is rooted in the idea that meaning is made between people — not handed down from an expert in a chair.

In our work together we will pay attention to what is happening in the present: thoughts, sensations, patterns in relationships, and the small choices that quietly shape a life. The aim is not to fix you. It is to help you meet yourself a little more clearly.

## Training & credentials

- Registered psychologist (NIP)
- Gestalt therapy training, ongoing
- Member of the Dutch Association of Psychologists

## Languages

I work in English. Sessions can take place in person in Maastricht or online.
