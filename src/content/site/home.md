Living abroad changes things in ways that are hard to describe to friends back home. Whatever is feeling heavy, confusing, or stuck — there is a way through it, and you do not have to figure it out alone.

I work with adults and students who want a thinking space that is honest, considered, and grounded in the body as much as the mind. Sessions take place in Maastricht or online, in English.
