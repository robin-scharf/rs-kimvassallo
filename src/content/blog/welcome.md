This space exists for the writing I want to share between sessions: short notes, longer essays, occasional reading recommendations. Nothing here is therapy. It is closer to a public notebook — a way of thinking out loud about the work.

If something here speaks to you, I would love to hear about it. And if you are wondering whether to start your own work, the simplest first step is a short introductory call.
